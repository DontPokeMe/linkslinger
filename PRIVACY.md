# LinkSlinger Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** March 2025

LinkSlinger ("the extension") is a Chrome extension for selecting multiple links and performing bulk actions (open in tabs, copy, bookmark, export). This policy describes what data the extension stores and when any data is sent off your device.

## Data stored on your device

The extension stores data only in Chrome's local storage (on your device):

- **Settings:** Your preferences (profiles, actions, colors, filters, blocklist, and similar options) are stored in `chrome.storage.local`. They never leave your device.

We do not use tracking, analytics, or any third-party SDKs. The extension does not collect or transmit any data by default.

## When data is sent off your device

The extension does **not** send any data off your device. It makes no network requests of its own. All actions (link selection, opening tabs or windows, copying to clipboard, creating bookmarks, and saving settings) happen locally via Chrome's extension APIs.

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for advertising or marketing.

## Chrome Web Store and hosting

If you install the extension from the Chrome Web Store, Google's Chrome Web Store policies and Google Privacy Policy also apply to the installation and update process. The extension itself does not report usage or analytics to us or to Google beyond what the Chrome Web Store may collect for install/update counts.

## Changes

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will be revised when we do. Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
